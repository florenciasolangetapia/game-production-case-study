# Game Brief

**Project:** La Última Tanda  
**Prepared by:** Florencia Solange Tapia  
**Role:** Producer / Project Manager  
**Status:** Pre-production portfolio case study

## High Concept

*La Última Tanda* is a cozy narrative management game set in a small Buenos Aires bakery after closing time. The player completes the final orders of the day while managing ingredients, time, and conversations with recurring customers.

## Intended Player Experience

The game combines the satisfaction of completing practical tasks with the emotional connection of a character-driven story. Each decision should create gentle pressure without turning the experience into a traditional time-management game.

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Review pending orders and available ingredients.
2. Decide which tasks to prioritize.
3. Prepare and deliver each order before closing.
4. Interact with customers through short dialogue choices.
5. Review the consequences at the end of the shift.

## Vertical Slice Scope

The first playable slice will include:

- One complete 20–25 minute shift
- One bakery environment
- Three recurring customers
- Four playable recipes
- Basic ingredient and time management
- Two possible end-of-shift outcomes

## Out of Scope

The vertical slice will not include:

- Online multiplayer
- Character customization
- An open-world environment
- A complete progression economy
- The full narrative campaign

## Target Audience

Players who enjoy cozy games, narrative experiences, light management systems, and character-focused stories.

## Target Success Criteria

- Players understand the core loop within the first five minutes.
- At least 80% of playtesters complete the shift without assistance.
- No progression-blocking defects remain in the test build.
- Players can identify at least one meaningful consequence of their decisions.
